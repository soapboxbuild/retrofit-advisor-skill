Build a multi-year decarbonization capex plan:

1. Baseline: current energy use, emissions, CRREM position
2. Target: compliance year, stranding risk mitigation, BEPS requirements
3. Year 1-2: operational and low-cost measures (retro-cx, lighting, controls)
4. Year 3-5: systems upgrades (VFDs, BMS, insulation)
5. Year 5+: major equipment replacement (chillers, heat pumps, solar)
6. For each year: capex required, projected savings, cumulative carbon reduction
7. IRA credits and incentives timeline
8. Progress toward CRREM pathway alignment

Present as a year-by-year roadmap with cumulative cost and carbon charts.
