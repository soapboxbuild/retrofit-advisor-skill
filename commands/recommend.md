Generate AI-ranked retrofit recommendations for this building:

1. Assess current building systems (HVAC, lighting, envelope, controls) from uploaded documents or data
2. Identify applicable measures from each tier (operational, systems, equipment)
3. For each measure: estimate cost, annual savings, payback, CO2 reduction
4. Calculate carbon abatement cost ($/tCO2e) for each measure
5. Align recommendations to the hold period
6. Check applicable IRA tax credits and utility incentives
7. Rank by: (a) lowest payback, (b) lowest carbon abatement cost, (c) required for regulatory compliance
8. Present as a prioritized capex roadmap with phasing
