# Decarbonization Planning Workflow

## Steps

1. **Baseline** — current EUI, carbon intensity, CRREM position, BEPS compliance status
2. **Measure identification** — survey applicable Tier 1/2/3 measures for this building type
3. **Ranking** — rank by payback, carbon abatement cost, compliance impact
4. **IRA screen** — identify applicable tax credits and incentives
5. **Phased plan** — design Year 1-3 / Year 3-7 / Year 7+ roadmap aligned to hold period
6. **Output** — capex plan artifact with year-by-year cost, savings, and carbon reduction

Pause after baseline and after ranking for review.
